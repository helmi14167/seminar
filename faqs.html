Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I register to vote? You can register to vote by visiting the Register/Login page and creating an account.
  • Can I nominate myself? Yes, students are free to nominate themselves for any position they qualify for.
  • What are the voting hours? Voting opens at 9:00 AM and closes at 8:00 PM on the day of the election.
